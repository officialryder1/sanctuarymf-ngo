Core Goals

Empowerment


At the heart of our foundation lies a deep commitment
to empowering individuals from all walks of life.

  • We believe that empowering people with knowledge, skills,
    and resources not only enhances their lives but also
    uplifts entire communities.
  • Through various programs, workshops, and educational initiatives,
    we aim to equip individuals with the tools they
    need to create a brighter future for
    themselves and their families

Development

We recognize that sustainable development is essential for the growth and prosperity of any society.

  • Our foundation is dedicated to identifying and implementing development
    projects that address the most pressing challenges faced by communities.
    From healthcare and education to infrastructure and economic development
  • we work tirelessly to foster sustainable change and improve the overall
    quality of life for everyone.

Education

We believe education is the key to unlocking potential and breaking the cycle of poverty. Through scholarships, school support programs, and educational workshops, we strive to enhance access to quality education for underprivileged children and youths.

Economic Empowerment

We support entrepreneurship and skill development programs to empower individuals to create sustainable livelihoods and contribute to their community's economic growth.

Healthcare

Good health is the foundation of a thriving community. Our healthcare initiatives aim to improve access to medical facilities, provide health education, and support research and interventions in critical health areas.

Infrastructure and Community Development

We work towards improving infrastructure, sanitation, and living conditions in underserved areas, fostering community-driven development projects.

Mission

Our ultimate goal is to eradicate poverty and improve sustainablelivelihood.

Vision

To become the foremost vibrant socioeconomic development organization successful in building capacity, providing infrastructure, andimproving the quality of life of the rural dwellers so that they can becomeproductive partners in development.

Purpose

Poised to promote good governance and sustainable development in our target region. We believe that the present intrinsic under development, suffering and poverty level among rural community, women and youth can be affected in positive and progressive ways.

Meet Our Founder



Amb.Dr. Ken Nwakanma is a cleric, a renowned fashion designer and philanthropist.

Amb.Dr. Ken Nwakanma is currently the CEO of Sanctuary Wealth Multipurpose Foundation and Sanctuary Wealth Multipurpose Cooperative Society through which he carries out extensive humanitarian activities beyond restrictions on which he has received various awards and recommendations. He also doubles as the Lead Pastor for his local non-denominational church “the Sanctuary of God Christian Assembly’ and as well as the President and founder for his christian association for African ministers and an outreach arm of his ministry aimed at reconciliation and deliverance world-wide called the Caleb Deliverance Outreach Ministry International.
