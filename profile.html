Profile Of Professor Ken Nwakanma

Ambassador Professor Ken Nwakanma is a cleric, renowned fashion designer, and philanthropist. He was born on May 29, 1965, in Aba, Abia State, Nigeria, and acquired his basic education in the same city. At 16, he began his career in couture, which quickly grew to employ over 12 professional fashion designers and numerous auxiliary staff, with three mega showrooms in Aba.



Amb.Dr. Ken Nwakanma is a cleric, a renowned fashion designer and philanthropist.

In 1993, he accepted the call to ministry and attended the New Dimension Seminary School in Lagos, Nigeria. He was awarded the Eminent Peace Ambassador to the United Nations on March 21, 2021, and received an Honorary Doctorate Degree from Cornerstone University and Theological Seminary, Jerusalem, Israel, and the USA, on March 28, 2021. On March 30, 2024, he received the Professorship laureate conferment on Peace and Conflict Management from Freedom University and Theological Seminary, Pottstown, USA.
Ambassador Professor Ken Nwakanma is the CEO of Sanctuary Wealth Multipurpose Foundation and Sanctuary Wealth Multipurpose Cooperative Society, through which he carries out extensive humanitarian and philanthropic efforts. He pioneered and serves as Lead Pastor of "The Sanctuary of God Christian Assembly" and is the President and founder of the Christian Association for African Ministers. He also presides over the Caleb Deliverance Outreach Ministry International.
He has held various positions, including Vice President of TELPECON (2020-2021), Federal Capital Territory Ambassador and Executive for IHRPS, and member of the International Parliament for Safety, Peace, and Justice. He serves as Global Ambassador for West Africa and is the country director of the United African Alliance Grant (UAAG) project. Ambassador Professor Ken Nwakanma is a Lieutenant in the Chaplaincy at Lazarus Union International, South East Zonal Commandant of the Chaplaincy, and a member of the US Chaplaincy. He is President of Global Empowerment Initiative Nigeria and the African Gospel Ministers Network (AGMN), with recognition from Nigerian Humanitarian Action (NIHA), receiving the International Social Justice Medal certificate. He is an author, capacity development expert, excellent leader, mentor, and father. He is courageous, has a positive mindset, and has the zeal to achieve high standards in all life's endeavors. Ambassador Professor Ken Nwakanma is happily married with six children and resides in Abuja, Nigeria.
